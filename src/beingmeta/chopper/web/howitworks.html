How the Chopper Works

The parser uses a weighted grammar and lexicon, searching for interpretations of individual words or word combinations which minimize the accumulated weight, or tension for the sentence.

Weights can be attached to paths in the grammar or to the interpretation of individual words or compounds. This allows the grammar itself to be quite general and adaptable while still handling the many idiosyncracies and special cases of human natural language.
